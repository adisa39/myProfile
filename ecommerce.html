Al-yusroh Ecommerce Website

Al-yusroh is an e-commerce online clothing store project that can be used to sell clothing materials and wrappers of different categories. Customers can buy shop and buy wrappers for their styling. The unique Selling point of the website is ability of the customer to be able to make request based on specific sample provided while the seller do the searching, it helps connect the seller to many potential customers. Customers can search, check and make payment online using different payment method. The website also help customers connect to stylists by choosing from different fashion design provided by experienced stylist. Chatting App is also incoporated with platform a better customer relation mangement. The objective;
Store can reach many online customers from the comfort of her store while while customer also shop for fabrics and wrappers at their conviniences.


Technologies: MDbootstrap, Javascript, Ajax, JQuery, HTML, CSS for front-end.
DJANGO, JSON, PYTHON, HUROKU for back-end integration.
